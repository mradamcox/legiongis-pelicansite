background

technology

We have a firm foundation in the ArcGIS software suite, but in recent years have become proficient with many open source and web-GIS software alternatives. Quantum GIS, GIMP, GDAL, GeoServer, OpenLayers 3, and Python are all firmly stowed in our toolbox. We also excel at research and data migration: finding, converting, and utilizing information from the depths of your institution or from your musty local government archives.

personnel

Adam Cox holds a BA in Anthropology from Lawrence University, in Appleton, Wisconsin. He has worked with GIS since 2011, beginning in cultural resource management and historic preservation, but finding work in many different fields--from hazard mitigation planning to co-teaching software classes. In his free time, he pores over old maps and gazes adoringly at remnants of old roads.
